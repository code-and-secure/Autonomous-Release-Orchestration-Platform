# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful,
harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and collaborate openly.
- Give and accept feedback professionally.
- Focus on what is best for the community.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful language.
- Personal attacks or trolling.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Any conduct that is inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of
acceptable behavior.

## Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to project
maintainers via the security contact process in SECURITY.md.

Maintainers may take corrective action, including warning, temporary ban, or
permanent ban depending on severity.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces and public interactions
related to this repository.
